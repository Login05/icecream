ABOUT

BOOMERANG! The year 1998 was very special to ice-cream lovers of Coimbatore City. A new concept store for ice-cream was born in the city. Vita brands India Private Limited, through Franchise Model, opened City's first Boomerang ice-cream store, a leading Ice-cream brand in Australia.Boomerang was the talk-of-the-city, as it revolutionized the art of servicing Ice-creams. First outlet was built with passion and commitment by Vita brands. Customers were delighted with the best and first-of-its-kind of infrastructure and service. For the first time, an ice-cream store was equipped with imported machineries from Italy for manufacturing. Several ingredients of ice-creams were also imported from MEC3, a well-known brand for ice-cream in Italy.
